Common Questions

Quick answers before you message

How do I place an order?

Simply send a WhatsApp message to 0806 019 3495. Tell us what you're interested in, and we'll guide you through availability, pricing, and payment. No forms, no apps.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers and secure payment links. Once your order is confirmed, we'll send you the details. Your payment information is never stored.

How long does delivery take?

Lagos orders typically arrive within 1-2 days. Other Nigerian cities take 2-5 days depending on location. We'll give you a tracking estimate when you order.

Can I return or exchange items?

Yes. If an item arrives damaged or isn't what you expected, message us within 48 hours. We'll sort out a replacement or refund — no stress.

Is there a minimum order amount?

No minimum. Order one item or ten — we'll handle it the same way. Free delivery applies to Lagos orders above ₦10,000.
